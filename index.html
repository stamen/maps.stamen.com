You can help us track down problems by creating an issue in the maps.stamen.com repo.

When submitting a new issue, please:

  • Make sure there isn't an issue already submitted.
  • Include in the description:
    1. Current maps.stamen.com URL
    2. A detailed description of the problem
    3. Screenshots would be great too!
As of July 31, 2023, Stamen Map Tiles are served by Stadia Maps. Click here to learn more.

Toner

These high-contrast B+W (black and white) maps are the perfect backdrop for your colorful and eye-catching map content and have been our most popular map style to date. Available in seven flavors: standard toner, labels, lines, background, lite, dark, and blacklite.

Terrain

Orient yourself with our terrain maps, featuring hill shading and natural vegetation colors. Available in three flavors: standard terrain, labels, lines, and background.

Watercolor

Reminiscent of hand drawn maps, our watercolor maps apply raster effect area washes and organic edges over a paper texture to add warm pop to any map.
